taylor osborn

Born in Santa Fe, New Mexico, Taylor Osborn began studying photography at the age of 15, eventually carrying his studies to the College of Santa Fe in Santa Fe, New Mexico. His work has been shown at The Museum of Fine Art in Santa Fe, New Mexico, and can be found on the website of the Smithsonian Institution, and locally in his adopted hometown of Portland, Oregon.
